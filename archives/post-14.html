Documenting my life.

To expand my creative output, I'm beginning to explore various forms of writing, storytelling, and video. My intention: to serve as a public guide to remember where I've been, my thoughts, and the ways I've changed (for better or for worse)

The seemingly small, incremental changes in our daily lives often go unnoticed. they become so trivial, we often forget about the progress made. As the year end approaches, I always find myself questioning "what exaclty did I accomplish this year?". Changes tend to get lossed when not documented.

"I cannot remember the books I've read any more than the meals I have eaten; even so, they have made me.” —Ralph Waldo Emerson
