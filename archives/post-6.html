The one second difference.

I've been asking myself this recently: when was the last time I listened to respond vs. listened to understand.

Often times in conversations, I find myself listening just to figure out a way to respond, and in some situations, that's perfectly fine.

But I've found most times than not, actively listening to someone and being present leads to better conversations and better understandings of one another.

The difference maker? Taking ~one second to think and formulate your response instead of blabbing your unfiltered thoughts.
