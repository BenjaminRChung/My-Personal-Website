Building meaningful relationships.

The pandemic brought forward a new 'normal' all our lives. It's shown me the value of meaningful relationships.

So then, how do we build these relationships? We're often hesitant when meeting someone new, wearing a 'mask' so to speak.

And I've come to realize, the willingness to being vulnerable and show humility has fostered these types of relationships in my life.

It's not easy. It takes time, commitment from both parties, and with no doubt, it'll be uncomfortable.
