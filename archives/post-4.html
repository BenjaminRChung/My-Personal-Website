Maintaining structure over time.

Life can be extremely overwhelming at times, especially with an abundant amount of online information.

We need constants, or rather habits, in our lives to maintain structure in our lives.

I found these to be the most helpful: sleep, nutrition, and physical exercise. They sound extremely simple, yet I often need a simple reminder to maintain these three things. It serves as the foundation to maintaining focus and I have a long way to perfecting this.
