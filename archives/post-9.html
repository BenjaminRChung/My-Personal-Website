Compounding efforts.

Exponential growth is a process that increases quantity over time. It occurs when the instantaneous rate of change of a quantity with respect to time is proportional to the quantity itself.

Compound interest is a well established principle in how wealth is made. Playing the game takes patience. It's the same with effort.

Consistency supersedes all other attempts at effort, be it periodic and sporadic. Your returns become magnitudes larger than your inputs, hence the exponential curve.

Look for exponential curves everywhere. So many things we do have a linear input-output relationship. Why do things linearly?
